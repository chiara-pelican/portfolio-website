The research project

After my medical studies, I started a PhD project focussing on cardiology and psychiatry. This research is investigation long-term psychological (and cardiological) outcomes of adults who underwent an open-heart surgery in childhood. More specifically, all these patiens were diagnosed at birth with a structural anomaly of their heart which is defined as congetial heart disease. Whereas exists different types of congetial heart diseases, we focussed on the 5 most common ones which required surgery in early stage of life.
